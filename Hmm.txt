
### **2. Systemd Service for Admin Bot**

```bash
# /etc/systemd/system/admin-token-bot.service
[Unit]
Description=Admin Token Management Bot
After=network.target

[Service]
Type=simple
User=root
WorkingDirectory=/etc/xray/admin
ExecStart=/usr/bin/python3 /etc/xray/admin/admin_bot.py
Restart=always
RestartSec=3
Environment=PYTHONUNBUFFERED=1

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
